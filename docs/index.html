Teaching Portfolio
English Language Arts | Grades 7-12

After earning teaching certification in 2007 through my undergraduate program in English Education at Mansfield University, I earned my MA in English from Binghamton University (SUNY) while teaching one of the pilot seminars in what has become a SUNY-wide Writing Initiative Program. Since then, I have advanced to doctoral candidacy at the University of Southern California, remained current in issues of education through consistent publication and conference participation, and taught literature and composition to students from a wide range of backgrounds: adult learners at urban community colleges, international students enrolled in online distance education programs, and first-generation TRIO students transitioning from high school to college.
